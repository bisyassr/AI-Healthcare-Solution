Inside Suki's AI Engine

Suki's effectiveness is built on a sophisticated, multi-layered technology stack designed specifically for the complexities of healthcare. Explore the core components that power the assistant.

A Purpose-Built Hybrid Model

Suki combines proprietary, medically-tuned AI with a human-in-the-loop process to ensure unparalleled accuracy. This isn't a generic voice assistant; it's a clinical expert.

  • ●

    Proprietary ASR: Trained on millions of medical conversations, Suki's Automatic Speech Recognition engine understands complex terminology and diverse accents with industry-leading precision.

  • ●

    99% Intent Extraction: Advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) allows Suki to understand the context and intent behind a clinician's words, not just transcribe them.

  • ●

    Human-in-the-Loop: A dedicated Clinical Operations team works with AI agents to continuously evaluate and refine note quality, ensuring the system learns and adapts to real-world clinical nuances.

Dynamic, Multi-Model Intelligence

Suki doesn't rely on a single Large Language Model. Its intelligent LLM Manager selects the best model for each specific task, ensuring optimal performance, quality, and reliability.

  • ●

    Task-Optimal Selection: One LLM might excel at summarizing patient history, while another is better for generating an assessment. The LLM Manager dynamically routes tasks to the best model, drawing from a portfolio including Google Gemini and OpenAI.

  • ●

    Resilience & Quality: This multi-model approach prevents vendor lock-in and ensures consistent quality, minimizing disruptions from potential outages or performance drops from any single provider.

Engineered for Clinical Confidence

In healthcare, trust is non-negotiable. Suki is built with foundational features to ensure reliability, transparency, and security, actively mitigating risks like AI hallucinations.

  • ●

    Evidence-Based Linking: 97% of Suki's ambient notes are linked to a verifiable source, like the EHR or encounter transcript. This allows clinicians to trace the origin of any information, building auditability and trust.

  • ●

    Enterprise-Grade Security: Suki is fully HIPAA compliant and SOC 2 Type 2 certified with zero exceptions, employing best-in-class encryption for data in-transit and at-rest.

A True Clinical Co-Pilot

Suki is more than a dictation tool. It's a comprehensive assistant that handles a wide range of administrative tasks within a single, unified solution.

Ambient & Dictated Notes

Generates clinical notes by passively listening to conversations or through direct dictation, giving clinicians 100% control to review and edit.

Clinical Coding

Streamlines ICD-10 and HCC coding, saving time and improving accuracy to help capture incremental revenue.

Clinical Q&A

Answers patient-specific and general medical questions, from summarizing lab results to checking drug interactions.

Order Staging

Allows clinicians to efficiently stage orders by voice, integrating them directly with the EHR workflow.

Patient Summaries

Quickly generates comprehensive summaries of a patient's chart, providing essential context at a glance.

Suki Platform (SDK/APIs)

Enables other healthcare applications to embed Suki's powerful AI and ambient capabilities into their own solutions.

Deepest EHR Integrations

Suki's key differentiator is its profound, bidirectional integration with major EHRs. It reads chart data and writes finished notes back, eliminating copy-paste and creating a truly seamless workflow.
